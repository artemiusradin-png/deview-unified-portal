TE Credit Review & Borrower Decision Portal

Prepared for: Mr. Lam
Prepared by: DeView
Date: April 16, 2026

1. Project Overview

DeView proposes to build a secure internal web portal for Eden's Father that works around the client's existing TE Credit workflow. The portal will automatically refresh permitted TE Credit borrower information once per day, organize the data into a clear review interface, and support staff with AI-assisted borrower summaries and good / bad grade indicators.

TE Credit remains the credit-reference source. The DeView portal becomes the client's internal operating layer for search, review, decision support, history tracking, AI summaries, audit logs, and management visibility.

Automatic TE Credit access will be implemented through the approved method available to the client, such as API, export, or permitted automated access. All access should remain limited to authorised borrower inquiries and subject to TE Credit's terms and Hong Kong personal data requirements.

TE Credit 審核及借款人決策平台

準備予：林先生

1. 項目概覽

DeView 建議為 Eden's Father 建立一個安全嘅內部網上平台，配合客戶現有 TE Credit 工作流程。平台會每日自動更新獲准使用嘅 TE Credit 借款人資料，將資料整理成清晰嘅審核介面，並以 AI 輔助員工查看借款人摘要及好 / 壞評級指標。

TE Credit 仍然係信貸資料來源。DeView 平台會成為客戶內部營運層，用於搜尋、審核、決策支援、歷史記錄、AI 摘要、審計紀錄及管理層可視化。

自動存取 TE Credit 會透過客戶獲批准可用嘅方法實作，例如 API、匯出檔案，或獲准嘅自動化存取。所有存取都應只限於授權借款人查詢，並受 TE Credit 條款及香港個人資料要求約束。
